DATA SOURCES NOTICE

The pretrained model weights were trained on mixed-source bird image data.

Training sources may include:
- LaSBiRD: Large Scale Bird Recognition Dataset, IEEE DataPort, DOI: 10.21227/s3xd-2s66
- iNaturalist media, used according to their applicable licenses and restrictions
- eBird/Clements taxonomy as a taxonomic reference only

No raw training images are included or redistributed with this software.
No eBird or Macaulay Library media are included in the training data unless explicitly stated otherwise.
